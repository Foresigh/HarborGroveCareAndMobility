Harbor Grove Care & Mobility LLC provides non-emergency medical transportation (NEMT) services including but not limited to:

  • Wheelchair-accessible vehicle transport
  • Gurney and stretcher transport
  • Dialysis treatment transport
  • Hospital discharge transport
  • Senior and elderly transportation

4. Scheduling & Cancellation Policy

Cancellations

We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule rides due to vehicle unavailability, severe weather, road closures, or other circumstances beyond our control. We will make every reasonable effort to notify you as early as possible and assist in rescheduling.
