FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first ride.

Does Medicaid cover my ride?
Yes — we accept Idaho Medicaid and work with MTM (Medical Transportation Management). If you have Medicaid, your rides to covered medical appointments may be fully paid. Call us and we'll verify your coverage on the spot.
How far in advance do I need to book?
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee availability. However, we do our best to accommodate same-day requests — just call us and we'll let you know right away if we can help.
Can a family member ride along?
Yes. One attendant or family member may accompany the client at no additional charge, subject to vehicle capacity. Please mention this when booking so we can make the appropriate arrangements.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
We understand plans change. Please notify us at least 2 hours before your scheduled pickup to avoid any cancellation fees. Medicaid cancellation policies are governed by applicable program rules.
Do you provide return trips?
Absolutely. We can arrange round-trip transportation so you don't have to worry about finding a ride home after your appointment. Just let us know when booking and we'll schedule the return trip as well.
Are your vehicles wheelchair accessible?
Yes. Our fleet includes fully ADA-compliant vehicles with wheelchair ramps, securement systems, and adequate space for mobility devices. We also offer stretcher transport for clients who cannot sit upright.
What areas do you serve?
We currently serve Boise, Meridian, Nampa, Eagle, Caldwell, Kuna, Star, and surrounding Treasure Valley communities. Not sure if we cover your area? Call us at 208-297-3601 and we'll let you know right away.
What types of appointments do you cover?
We transport clients to any non-emergency medical appointment — doctor visits, dialysis, physical therapy, specialist consultations, lab work, imaging, hospital discharges, and more. If it's medical and non-emergency, we can help.
